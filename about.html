ABOUT HELP ETHIOPIA

HELP Ethiopia is a multidisciplinary, non-profit Organization based in Ethiopia that has been registered and accorded legal Personality by FDRE CSO Agency.

HELP Ethiopia was established by surgeons and scholars of different notable institutions with the objective of ensuring sustainable development in areas of health and education in Ethiopia. Our focus areas are improvement of health service delivery and training as well as the support of economically disadvantaged groups of our society.

We also advocate for quality education and better leadership as a tool for sustainable societal development.

Hence the names “HELP” which stands for Health, Education, Leadership and Partnership. We are introducing innovative, evidence-based tools to implement our projects and achieve our long- and short-term goals.
