About Us

HELP Ethiopia was established by surgeons and scholars of different notable institutions with the objective of ensuring sustainable development in areas of health and education in Ethiopia. Our focus areas are improvement of health service delivery and training as well as the support of economically disadvantaged groups of our society. We also advocate for quality education and better leadership as a tool for sustainable societal development. Hence the names “HELP” which stands for Health, Education, Leadership and Partnership. We are introducing innovative, evidence-based tools to implement our projects and achieve our long- and short-term goals.

Our Mission

we also aspire to:
To provide health and counseling services to the ill-privileged for free or minimal cost; to encourage and promote volunteer activities;
To promote and support organizations and individuals working in the field of Education and Leadership training.
To Collaborate with national and international governmental and non-governmental organizations in the field of health, education and related management issues.
In collaboration with involved stakeholders, to encourage health and educational institutions to contribute to sustainable development of the country

Our vision

Help Ethiopia also works to seed the initiation of a country where the health, peace, development and rights of the community are sustained. We also aspire to coordinate domestic knowledge and values with modern ideologies to make our country a prosperous African Nation.
